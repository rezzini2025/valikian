About

Let me introduce myself.

I am an accomplished Occupational Hygienist, Environmental Health Scientist, and Public Health professional with over four decades of international experience in occupational hygiene, industrial ventilation, and environmental risk management. Holding advanced degrees from leading institutions including Aston University (MPhil), the London School of Hygiene and Tropical Medicine (MSc), and Tehran University (MSPH, MSc, BSc).

My professional expertise spans research, consultancy, policy development, and training in areas such as air pollution control, workplace exposure assessment, industrial hygiene, and health risk evaluation. I have chaired numerous international and national scientific courses and conferences, published widely in peer-reviewed journals, and represented governmental and academic institutions at WHO, ACGIH, and international health and safety forums.
